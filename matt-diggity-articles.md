The Diggity Marketing Blog is Matt Diggity's primary educational resource, covering SEO, affiliate marketing, AI-assisted SEO workflows, technical SEO, link building, and real-world case studies. The blog focuses on practical experiments and data-driven strategies rather than theory, helping marketers understand how Google's algorithms evolve and how to build sustainable organic traffic.
