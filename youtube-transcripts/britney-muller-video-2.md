## Summary

In this presentation, Britney Muller explains how businesses can build an effective SEO strategy by focusing on high-impact activities rather than trying to optimize everything at once. She emphasizes understanding user intent, prioritizing content that addresses real customer problems, and using data to guide SEO decisions. ## Key Takeaways

- Focus on quality and relevance instead of publishing large amounts of content.

- Use search intent to guide content planning and keyword targeting.

- Prioritize SEO tasks that produce the greatest business impact.

- Analyze search data continuously to improve content performance.
