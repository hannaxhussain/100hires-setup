## Summary

In this video, Matt Diggity discusses how artificial intelligence is changing modern SEO workflows. He explains how AI tools can help with keyword research, content planning, drafting, and optimization while emphasizing that successful SEO still depends on human expertise and strategic decision-making. Rather than relying on AI to mass-produce articles, he recommends using AI to improve efficiency and then refining the content to ensure originality, accuracy, and value for readers. ## Key Takeaways

- AI should be used to speed up research and content creation, not replace expert knowledge.

- High-quality, human-edited content performs better than unedited AI-generated articles.

- Building topical authority is more effective than publishing large volumes of unrelated content.

- Understanding user intent is essential for creating content that ranks well.
