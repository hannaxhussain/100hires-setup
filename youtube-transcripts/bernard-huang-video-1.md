# How to Rank SEO Content in the Era of Generative AI

**Expert:** Bernard Huang (Clearscope)

**YouTube Link:** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZytMamXMG0M](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZytMamXMG0M)

---

## Summary

In this presentation, Bernard Huang, co-founder of Clearscope, discusses how generative AI is transforming SEO content creation and search optimization. He explains that while AI can significantly improve content production efficiency, ranking success still depends on creating authoritative, accurate, and user-focused content. Bernard highlights how AI should be used to support research, content optimization, and workflow automation rather than simply generating large volumes of articles. He also emphasizes that Google's evolving search systems continue to reward content that demonstrates expertise, relevance, and value to users.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Generative AI is a powerful tool for improving SEO workflows but should not replace human expertise.

- High-quality, authoritative content remains essential for achieving strong search rankings.

- AI-assisted content should be carefully reviewed, edited, and optimized before publication.

- Content optimization platforms such as Clearscope help improve topical relevance and search visibility.

- Successful SEO strategies combine AI efficiency with editorial oversight and a strong understanding of user intent.
