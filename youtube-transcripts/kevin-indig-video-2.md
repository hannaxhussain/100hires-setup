## Summary

In this interview, Kevin Indig discusses how artificial intelligence is reshaping SEO and organic growth. He explains that search is moving away from a click-based model toward AI-generated answers, making visibility, authority, and brand recognition more important than rankings alone. The conversation also covers how marketers should adapt their content strategies to AI-powered search experiences, including Google's AI Overviews and other large language model (LLM) search tools. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI Overviews are changing user behaviour by answering many questions directly in search results.

- Brands need to focus on becoming trusted sources that AI systems are likely to cite.

- Success should be measured by overall business impact and visibility, not only by search traffic.

- AI is valuable for research, outlining, and content optimisation, but human expertise remains essential for high-quality content.

- SEO strategies should extend beyond Google by building authority across platforms such as YouTube, Reddit, newsletters, and industry publications. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
