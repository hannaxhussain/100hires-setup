## Summary

In this presentation, Jamie Indigo explains how organizations can develop a defensive SEO strategy for the rapidly evolving world of AI-powered search. Using the Johari Window framework, Jamie demonstrates how businesses can identify what they know about their own brand versus what search engines and AI models understand. The session emphasizes proactively managing online information, improving structured content, and strengthening technical SEO so AI systems can accurately interpret and represent a brand. ## Key Takeaways

- AI search requires businesses to actively manage how their brand is represented online.

- Understanding the gaps between brand knowledge and AI understanding helps improve SEO strategy.

- Structured data, consistent information, and technical SEO improve AI interpretation of website content.

- Monitoring AI-generated search results helps identify inaccuracies and new optimization opportunities.
