# How AI is Reshaping Google Search and SEO

**Expert:** Lily Ray

**YouTube Link:** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2htSIT0HLjs](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2htSIT0HLjs)

---

## Summary

In this presentation, Lily Ray examines how Google's AI-powered search features are changing the SEO landscape. She discusses the impact of AI-generated search results on website traffic, content visibility, and user behaviour. Lily explains why businesses should focus on creating trustworthy, experience-based content that demonstrates expertise while adapting their SEO strategies to evolving search technologies. She also highlights the importance of monitoring Google's updates and continuously improving content quality to remain competitive in AI-driven search environments.

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI-powered search is changing how users interact with Google and discover information.

- High-quality, trustworthy content is becoming increasingly important for long-term SEO success.

- Demonstrating Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness (E-E-A-T) improves content credibility.

- Businesses should monitor Google's AI search updates and adapt their SEO strategies accordingly.

- AI should support content creation and optimization, but human expertise and editorial review remain essential.
