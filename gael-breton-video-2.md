## Summary

In this session, Gael Breton discusses how artificial intelligence is transforming SEO workflows and content production. He explains that AI can dramatically improve efficiency in keyword research, outlining, content briefs, and workflow automation, but it should not replace human expertise or original thinking. Gael emphasizes building topical authority, producing genuinely helpful content, and creating scalable systems that combine AI with editorial review. ## Key Takeaways

- AI is most valuable when it supports research, planning, and content optimization rather than replacing human writers.

- Strong topical authority remains one of the most effective long-term SEO strategies.

- Businesses should create repeatable content systems instead of relying on one-off SEO tactics.
