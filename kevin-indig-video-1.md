## Summary

In this discussion, Kevin Indig explains how artificial intelligence is fundamentally changing search and SEO. He argues that SEO is evolving from simply ranking webpages to building trusted brands and creating content that answers user intent effectively. He emphasizes that AI-generated search experiences require marketers to rethink how they measure success, shifting focus from traffic alone to business outcomes, authority, and visibility across multiple channels. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Key Takeaways

- AI is transforming how users search for and discover information.

- Traditional SEO metrics such as clicks and rankings are becoming less important than overall visibility and business impact.

- Strong brands are more likely to earn trust in AI-assisted search experiences.

- Companies should diversify beyond Google by investing in platforms such as YouTube, Reddit, newsletters, and communities.

- AI should be used to improve research, content creation, and content repurposing—not simply to generate large volumes of low-quality content. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
