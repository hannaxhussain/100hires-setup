## Summary

In this presentation, Britney Muller explains how featured snippets work and why they are an important opportunity for increasing organic visibility. She discusses how Google selects content for featured snippets and demonstrates techniques for structuring content so that search engines can easily identify concise, authoritative answers. Although the talk predates the recent wave of generative AI search experiences, many of the principles—clear content structure, semantic relevance, and satisfying user intent—remain directly applicable to AI-powered SEO and AI-generated search results. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Key Takeaways

- Structure content with clear headings and concise answers to improve discoverability.

- Focus on answering user intent instead of simply targeting keywords.

- Well-organized content increases the likelihood of appearing in enhanced search results.

- Semantic relevance and contextual information help search engines understand content.

- AI-powered search still rewards content that is accurate, authoritative, and easy to interpret. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
