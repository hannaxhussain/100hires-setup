### Summary

Lily Ray's articles on the Amsive Insights platform focus on the evolution of search in the age of artificial intelligence. She explains how AI-powered search, Answer Engine Optimization (AEO), and generative search experiences are changing SEO while emphasizing that the core principles of SEO remain the same. Rather than chasing every new AI trend, Lily encourages marketers to prioritize trustworthy content, technical SEO, strong brand authority, and user-focused experiences. Her work also explores Google algorithm updates, AI search visibility, and practical strategies for adapting SEO to large language models and AI-powered search engines. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

### Key Insights

- AI search expands traditional SEO rather than replacing it.

- Strong E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness) remains one of the most important ranking factors.

- High-quality, well-structured content improves visibility in both Google Search and AI-powered search engines.

- Brands should monitor AI search visibility using new performance metrics beyond traditional rankings.

- Technical SEO, structured content, and answering user questions clearly continue to be essential for long-term success. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
