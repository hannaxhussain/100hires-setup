### Summary

The Growth Memo is Kevin Indig's weekly newsletter focused on organic growth, SEO, AI, and product-led marketing. It explores how artificial intelligence is transforming search behavior, content production, and digital marketing strategies. Rather than focusing only on rankings, the newsletter emphasizes building topical authority, understanding user intent, and measuring business outcomes. Kevin regularly combines industry research, real-world case studies, and practical frameworks to help marketers adapt to AI-powered search.

### Key Insights

- AI is fundamentally changing how users discover information online.

- SEO should focus on business impact rather than rankings alone.

- Topical authority and content depth are becoming increasingly important.

- AI should assist research and content creation while human expertise ensures quality.

- Diversifying traffic sources beyond Google strengthens long-term organic growth.
