# Google Search & AI: Understanding the Future of SEO

**Expert:** Lily Ray

**YouTube Link:** [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9dONdby7dDU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9dONdby7dDU)

---

## Summary

In this presentation, Lily Ray explains how Google's increasing use of artificial intelligence is changing the search landscape and what SEO professionals need to do to remain visible. She discusses the importance of creating trustworthy, experience-based content, adapting to Google's AI-powered search features, and focusing on long-term authority rather than short-term ranking tactics. The session also highlights how AI is changing user behaviour and why businesses should continually evaluate how their content appears in AI-driven search experiences.

---

## Key Takeaways

- AI-powered search is changing how users discover information online.

- High-quality, trustworthy content is more important than ever for maintaining visibility.

- Demonstrating expertise, experience, authority, and trust (E-E-A-T) helps content perform better in evolving search environments.

- SEO should focus on solving user problems instead of relying on outdated optimization tactics.

- Businesses should continuously monitor search changes and adapt their content strategies as AI-powered search evolves.
