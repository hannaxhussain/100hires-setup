## Summary

In this SEO Masterclass, Gael Breton shares his approach to succeeding in SEO during the era of AI and evolving Google algorithms. He explains how Authority Hacker combines high-quality content, strategic link building, topical authority, and technical SEO to build sustainable websites. Rather than relying solely on AI-generated content, Gael advocates using AI as a productivity tool for research, outlining, and workflow improvements while ensuring that human expertise and editorial review remain central to content creation. ## Key Takeaways

- AI should enhance SEO workflows rather than replace human expertise.

- Building topical authority with comprehensive, high-quality content is more effective than publishing large volumes of AI-generated articles.

- High-quality backlinks, digital PR, and strong brand signals remain essential for long-term SEO success.

- Diversifying traffic sources through platforms like Pinterest, Google Discover, Reddit, and YouTube reduces dependence on Google Search alone.

- E-E-A-T, trust signals, and technical SEO continue to play an important role in improving visibility within AI-powered search environments. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
